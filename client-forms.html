Consent to Services

Description of Services

Open Grace LLC is a Coordinated Family Support (CFS) provider operating under contract with Inland Counties Regional Center (IRC). CFS services assist adults with developmental disabilities who live in their family home by providing personalized coordination, service navigation, and advocacy. Services include assessment of service needs, development and monitoring of service plans, coordination of community resources, advocacy, and ongoing family support.

Authorization & Hours

Services are provided pursuant to a Purchase of Service (POS) authorized by IRC. The authorized service hours and scope are outlined in the consumer's Individual Program Plan (IPP). Open Grace will provide services within the authorized hours only. Any changes to service authorization require IRC approval.
I consent to Open Grace LLC providing Coordinated Family Support services to the consumer named above as authorized by Inland Counties Regional Center.
I understand that services are provided within the hours authorized on the Purchase of Service (POS) and that additional hours require IRC approval.
I understand that I may withdraw consent for services at any time by notifying Open Grace LLC and IRC in writing, without affecting the consumer's eligibility for other regional center services.

Release of Information

Open Grace LLC may need to share information about the consumer with Inland Counties Regional Center, the consumer's service coordinator, and other authorized service providers. All information is shared only to the extent necessary to coordinate services and is protected under Welfare & Institutions Code §4514.
I authorize Open Grace LLC to exchange information with Inland Counties Regional Center and the assigned Service Coordinator as necessary to coordinate and document services.
I authorize Open Grace LLC to share information with other service providers directly involved in the consumer's care as needed to coordinate services.
I understand that all information shared is confidential and protected under W&I Code §4514 and applicable federal privacy laws.

Client Rights & Confidentiality

Acknowledgment of Consumer Rights — W&I Code §4500 et seq.

Consumer Information

Your Rights as a Regional Center Consumer

As a consumer receiving services through Inland Counties Regional Center and Open Grace LLC, you have the following rights under the Lanterman Developmental Disabilities Services Act (W&I Code §4500 et seq.):
  • The right to receive services in the least restrictive environment appropriate to your needs
  • The right to be treated with dignity and respect at all times
  • The right to participate in the development of your Individual Program Plan (IPP)
  • The right to have your personal information kept confidential
  • The right to file a complaint or grievance without fear of retaliation
  • The right to request a fair hearing if you disagree with a regional center decision
  • The right to have an advocate or representative of your choice present at any meeting
  • The right to refuse services without losing eligibility for other regional center services
  • The right to access your records upon request

Confidentiality

All information about you and your family is strictly confidential and protected under Welfare & Institutions Code §4514. Open Grace LLC will not share your personal information with anyone outside of your service team without your written consent, except as required by law.

Grievance & Complaint Process

If you have a concern or complaint about the services you receive from Open Grace LLC you may:
  • Speak directly with your assigned CFS Family Coordinator
  • Contact the Open Grace director at joshua.kennedy@opengrace.org
  • Contact your Inland Counties Regional Center Service Coordinator
  • Request a fair hearing through IRC by calling (951) 241-8102
  • Contact the Office of Clients' Rights Advocacy (OCRA) for independent advocacy
All complaints will be addressed promptly and without retaliation.

Mandatory Reporting

Open Grace LLC staff are mandated reporters under California law. Staff are required to immediately report any suspected abuse, neglect, or exploitation of a consumer to the appropriate authorities. This reporting obligation cannot be waived.

I understand the grievance and complaint process and how to exercise the consumer's rights if needed.
I understand that Open Grace LLC staff are mandated reporters and are required by law to report suspected abuse or neglect.
