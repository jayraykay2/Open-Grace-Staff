Step 1 — Before You Begin
1
Complete the Onboarding Form
Submit your employment documents, acknowledgements, and certifications through Jotform. Required before your first service contact.
Open Onboarding Form →

Live Scan Fingerprinting
You must complete Live Scan fingerprinting before working with any consumer. Open Grace will reimburse this cost after 30 days of active employment with your original receipt.
Download Live Scan Request Form →
3
CPR / First Aid Certification
A current CPR/First Aid certification is required before client contact. Open Grace will reimburse this cost after 30 days of active employment with your original receipt.
Step 2 — Review & Sign
4
Read the Employee Handbook
Review the full Employee Handbook covering all Open Grace policies, procedures, and expectations. You will sign an acknowledgement in your onboarding form.
Read Handbook →
5
Complete Mandated Reporter Training
As a Family Coordinator you are a mandated reporter under California law. Complete the state-approved training and submit your certificate in the onboarding form.
Step 3 — Systems Access
6
Microsoft Teams & 365 Setup

7
Daily Contact Log — Test Submission
Submit a test entry to the daily contact log using your staff access code. The Director will confirm your submission went through successfully.
Open Contact Log →
8
Coordinator Portal Access
Your personal coordinator portal shows your assigned clients, training progress, and onboarding checklist. Log in with your staff access code.
Open Coordinator Portal →

9
Complete Day 1 Training Modules
Complete all four Day 1 required modules before your first client contact: Title 17 & CFS, HIPAA, Mandated Reporter, and Open Grace Policies. Each has a quiz — score 80%+ to earn your certificate.
