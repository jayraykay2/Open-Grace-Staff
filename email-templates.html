📋 Referral Acknowledgment
👤 Staff Onboarding
When to send this email Send within 2 business days of receiving a referral from an IRC Service Coordinator. Confirms receipt, sets expectations for the client and family, and establishes your first point of contact. Fill in the highlighted fields before sending.

Subject: Referral Acknowledgment — [Client Name] | UCI [UCI #] | Open Grace CFS
Open Grace LLC
Coordinated Family Support Services
Dear [SC Name],

Thank you for your referral. Open Grace LLC is pleased to confirm receipt of the following referral and looks forward to supporting this client and family.

Referral Details
Client Name: [Client Name]
UCI Number: [UCI #]
Referral Date: [Referral Date]
Assigned CFS Staff: [Staff Name]

Our assigned CFS Family Coordinator will make initial contact with the client's family within 5 business days of this acknowledgment. We will review all provided documentation including the IPP and CDER prior to the first contact.

Please note the following regarding our service timeline:

  • Initial family contact will be made within 5 business days
  • The initial assessment will be scheduled in coordination with the family
  • Quarterly reports will be submitted per IRC guidelines — by the 1st of the month following each 90-day interval
  • You will be copied on any significant updates or concerns
Assigned Staff Contact Information
Name: [Staff Name]
Phone: [Staff Phone]
Email: [Staff Email]

If you have any questions, updates, or additional documentation to share, please do not hesitate to reach out. We value our partnership with Inland Counties Regional Center and are committed to providing quality coordinated family services.
